Short reflection

Unit activities

I explored a SCRUM solution proposed by Pohl and Hof (2015), where the add 4 new categories in the normal SCRUM to identify security issues during software development. Let's explore them:

  • IDENTIFICATION: identify security issues, these are marked in the Product Backlog of Scrum.
  • IMPLEMENTATION: raise awareness of the SCRUM team for security during sprint.
  • VERIFICATION: testing.
  • DEFINITION OF DONE: check timeline for identified and tackled issue and focus on problems the team is not aware of.

See how each category is related to the original SCRUM categories in Fig. 1.
