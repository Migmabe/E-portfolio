Unit 1

Introduction to Secure Software Development

Short reflection

I start the module with great motivation, hoping for a more enriching experience than in the last. This module aims to give me the necessary basic tools and knowledge to follow secure development practices. Several attacks will be explored, and the coursework seems rather interesting with group coursework and individual system implementation based on that document.

This week we refreshed concepts in UML and visited some of the standards which we will use throughout the module. These are the OWASP (Open Web Application Security Project) and MITRE. The lecturecast also introduced me to some approaches to develop software; SDLC (Software Development Life Cycle).

I also created my first post in the forum. Here, I identify broken access control as per OWASP and developed a couple of UML models that show how to mitigate it and what can lead to the weakness ocurring.

I am quite excited of the way forward!
