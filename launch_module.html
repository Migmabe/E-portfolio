Reflective piece

Postgraduate education is a major step forward in a personâ€™s professional development. Adapting to a new way of understanding and critical thinking is not an easy step. Launching to Computer Science adds another level of complexity, with a topic I had never touched.


I took on this challenge as part of a redefined professional journey. My goal is to get into the Aerial Robotics industry, intersecting computer science with mechanical and aeronautics knowledge to hopefully develop autonomous aero systems. In previous experience, I had learned that I lack on study planning, tangling up on topics that were sometimes out of scope. With this part time module, I have improved on this aspect and performed to a great standard while working part time in a demanding environment. This was partially achieved thanks to following strict deadlines and being extremely consistent. Moreover, the course structure helped in this regard. During this 12-week module I have not only improved my soft and management skills, but also acquired relevant knowledge in Python, MySQL, HTML, CSS and working with GitHub workflows. These were put into practice with 4 assignments.


Panadero, Jonsson and Botella (2017) proved that student self-assessment and self-guided learning improves their efficiency studying. Postgraduate studies require a lot of self-guidance. Everything depends on what you achieve, who you reach and how you plan. We are our harshest judges and several areas are to be significantly improved. These are algorithm understanding and testing.
